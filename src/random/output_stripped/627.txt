5.511 How can logic--all-embracing logic, which mirrors the world--use such peculiar crotchets and contrivances? Only because they are connected with such rules: it is impossible for words to appear in two dimensions. Indeed, it exists in one-dimensional space in which right and left etc. are not primitive signs.
